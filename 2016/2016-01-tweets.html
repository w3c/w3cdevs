Web Developers Meetup (CSS, SVG, Houdini)

The CSS and SVG Working Groups (the folks responsible for the ongoing development of some of your favourite web technologies) are meeting in Sydney in early February, and would love to get the chance to connect with the Australian Web Design and Development community.   So, on the evening of February 3, Google will be hosting a meetup, with presentations from a number of the members of these working groups, to give you an even better sense of where these technologies are headed. Speakers will include Fantasai: CSS Best Practices from the CSSWG Rossen Atanasov & Shane Stephens: Houdini - the most exciting development in CSS you've never heard of Tavmjong Bah: Future directions for SVG Jihye Hong: Introducing CSS Round Display Greg Whitworth: Introducing CSS Grid Plus there'll be a drink or two and a bit to eat as well. It's all free of course, just RSVP, and put February 3 in your diary. ------- Also organized by John Allsopp (@johnallsopp) For nearly 20 years John Allsopp developed software and written books, courses, tutorials, and articles for web designers and developers.  He speaks not infrequently on all things web in Australia and around the world. He's one of the founders of the Web Directions Conferences.
